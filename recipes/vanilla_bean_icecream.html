Vanilla Bean Ice Cream

Description

This vanilla bean ice cream recipe creates a rich, creamy, and flavorful dessert that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean, split and scraped
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine the milk, cream, sugar, vanilla bean, and salt.
  2. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Remove from heat and let the mixture steep for 30 minutes.
  4. Strain the mixture and chill it in the refrigerator.
  5. Churn the mixture in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  6. Transfer the ice cream to a container and freeze it for at least 4 hours.
