Pancakes

Description

This pancake recipe yields fluffy, delicious pancakes that are perfect for breakfast or brunch. They are easy to make and can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsps melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the egg and milk.
  2. In another bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, then add melted butter and mix until smooth.
  4. Let the batter rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet and cook until golden brown (about 2-3 minutes).
  7. Flip and cook for another 2-3 minutes until done.
  8. Serve with butter and maple syrup.
