Mac and Cheese

Description

This mac and cheese recipe is a classic, creamy, and cheesy comfort food that's easy to make and perfect for any meal.

Ingrediants

  • 1 pound pasta (elbow macaroni or cavatappi)
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter and whisk in the flour.
  3. Cook the mixture for 2 minutes, then gradually whisk in the milk.
  4. Add the salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder.
  5. Cook the mixture until it thickens, then remove from heat.
  6. Stir in the shredded cheeses until melted.
  7. Combine the cooked pasta and cheese sauce.
  8. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20-25 minutes.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
